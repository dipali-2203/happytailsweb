About Us

Built by pet lovers, for pet lovers — because every wag, purr, or chirp matters.

We make sure your pets get the care they deserve

Happy Tails started with a simple idea: to give pet parents the power of tech to support everyday pet needs — all in one app. From scheduling to reminders and expert advice, our platform was built to simplify and elevate how you care for your furry family.

“Making pet care personal, practical, and playful.”
— Happy Tails Founder

Our Vision

We aim to be the most trusted companion for pet care in every home. With innovative features, a caring community, and 24/7 support, we're redefining what it means to be a responsible pet parent. Our platform aspires to connect pet parents with trustworthy services, real-time support, and a growing community of animal lovers. From the smallest pawprints to the biggest wagging tails, we're here to make every pet’s life happier, healthier, and filled with love.
